You are being asked to take part in a research study being done by Coby Weiss, a student at Harvard University. It is your choice whether or not to participate in this research. If you choose to participate, you may change your mind and leave the study at any time by closing the webpage.

If you choose to be in the study, you will complete a questionnaire. This questionnaire will help us learn more about the meaning of English sentences. The questionnaire will take you around 20 minutes to complete. Please note that you can participate only if you are an adult native English speaker.

If you participate in this study, you will have the opportunity to think about your own language. There are no foreseeable risks from participating in this study.

The data we collect from this survey is confidential. Please do not include your name or other information that could be used to identify you in the questionnaire responses. Aggregate, de-identified data may be made available to researchers outside of the research team associated with this project.
